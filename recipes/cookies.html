Ooey Gooey Double Chocolate Chip Cookies

The name says it all! This is your standard cookie recipe jammed packed with 2 kinds of chocolate chips. I like to use Ghirardelli 60% cacao dark chocolate chips and semi-sweet chocolate chips. This is my preference so feel free to experiment with different brands and kinds. My husband likes white chocolate so I will change it up at time and use the dark chocolate and white chocolate.

    Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 C Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 C Butter (room temp)
  • 1/2 C Brown Sugar
  • 6 Tblsp Granulated Sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 kinds of chocolate chips (About 1 1/4 C each)

    Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees and line baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt in bowl and set aside
  3. Using an electric mixer on low, Beat together butter and sugar until smooth
  4. Add in Egg and Vanilla and mix until combined
  5. Slowly add flour mix, a little at a time scrapping the sides of the mixer, mix until incorporated being sure not to overmix
  6. Fold in chocolate chips being sure not to overmix
  7. Drop cookie dough pieces onto baking sheet
  8. Bake for 8-12 mins until Golden brown
  9. Allow to cool a couple mins then transfer to wire rake
